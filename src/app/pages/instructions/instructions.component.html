Instructions

Step 1: Configure your account to either a teacher or moderator. If you are a teacher, you will also need to add at least 1 of your moderators.

Step 2a: As a teacher, you can create a moderation batch (Please note: only pdf files can be uploaded for moderation. You can find a free tool to convert files to pdf here) and when the batch is moderated, view feedback on it.
Step 2b: As a moderator, you can view a moderation batch, add annotations to the files in the batch and then provide feedback to the teacher.
